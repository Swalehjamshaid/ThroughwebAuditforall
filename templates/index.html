Frequently Asked Questions

How do daily emails work?

Set your timezone and preferred hour. The scheduler runs audits for scheduled sites and emails you a certified report link.

Do you check Core Web Vitals?

Yes. Add PSI_API_KEY in environment to enable Lighthouse/Core Web Vitals via Google PSI.

Is it secure?

Passwords are hashed (bcrypt), email verification is required, sessions use signed HttpOnly cookies, and access is role-based.

Where is data stored?

On Railway’s managed PostgreSQL (or SQLite locally). Audits, trend history, and user/site configs are persisted.
